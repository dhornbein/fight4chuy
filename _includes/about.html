About

Fight4chuy is an independent effort to bring skilled campaign volunteers to Chicago to help "Chuy" Garcia defeat Rahm Emanuel.

  • Join us in Chicago

    We are looking for folks who can travel on their own dime to Chicago for at least four days, preferably for a week. We will house and feed you while making sure your talents and enthusiasm are well used.

  • We're looking for a wide range of skills

    don't think that the lack of highly specialized or technical campaign skills means Chuy doesn't need your help! Among the skills we are looking for:

    • Graphic design
    • Copywriting
    • Translation
    • Web design and coding
    • VAN / Turf cutting
    • Photography
    • Video shooting / editing / production
    • Field and canvassing, esp. experience managing other volunteers
    • Social media

    Who we are

    This effort is organized by veterans of the Zephyr Teachout campaign, who fought Gov. Cuomo in a primary last year. Cuomo is our Rahm. It's clear to us that defeating Rahm Emanuel would be a wonderful thing with a national impact - and we want to help. We're in touch with the right people in the Garcia campaign. Our goal is to facilitate the efforts of out of town, skilled campaign volunteers from across the land. There's no way we're gonna let Chuy win without us!

    We are proud to be working with Occupy Wall Street, and other partners. Stay tuned!
